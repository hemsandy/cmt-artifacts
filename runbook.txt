#How to start Active MQ
  . Login to zookeeper
  . change dir to activemq-../bin
  . activemq start
  
#How to start Storm
 . Start zookeeper
 	under bin of zookeeper ./zkserver.sh start
 . Start nimbus
 	under bin of storm ./storm nimbus
 . Start supervisor
 	under bin of storm ./storm supervisor
